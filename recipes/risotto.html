Risotto

Description

Everything is pre-cut and pre-seasoned, making lasagna incredibly easy to prepare!

Ingredients

Meat sauce

  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Carrot
  • Celery
  • Beef
  • Canned tomato
  • Tomato paste
  • Red wine
  • Beef bouillon cubes
  • Bay leaves
  • Thyme
  • Oregano
  • Worcestershire sauce

White sauce

  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese

For assemble

  • Lasagne sheets
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. we cook that meat sauce long and slow, to develop incredible rich flavours and make the beef melt-in-your-mouth tender.
  2. Smear a bit of meat sauce on the base first – stops the lasagna sheets from sliding around
  3. Layer 1 – top with meat sauce, bit of white sauce
  4. Layer 2 – lay out more lasagna sheets, then top with more meat sauce and more white sauce
  5. Layer 3 – repeat again, lasagna sheets, meat sauce then white sauce; and
  6. Topping – cover with lasagna sheets, pour over remaining white sauce then sprinkle with cheese.
  7. Pop it in the oven for 30 minutes
